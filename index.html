Our 23,000 square foot complex features thousands of high quality and competitively priced designer fabrics covering classic to trendy styles. We can also help you refinish your entire home with our in-house interior designer and on-site flooring and window centers.

Fabric Depot Design center has been turning houses into homes for more than 20 years. Located in Phoenix, Arizona, we serve homeowners, designers and businesses alike.

We work with some of Arizona's finest upholsterers, flooring and window installers, and fabric manufacturers to bring you the best service at the best prices. Let us know if you need help completing your project. We can assist you from start to finish.

We aim to become your one-stop-shop for interior design in Arizona. We aspire to deliver exceptional customer service and to create an at-home environment where your creativity can flourish.
